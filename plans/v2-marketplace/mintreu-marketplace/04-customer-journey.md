# Chapter 4: The Customer's Journey

This chapter outlines the experience of a customer interacting with the Mintreu Marketplace, from discovering products to managing their purchases and licenses.

## 4.1. Product Discovery: Finding the right product.

Customers will have a user-friendly interface to browse and search for digital products.

*   **Homepage**: Featuring new arrivals, popular products, and curated collections.
*   **Categories & Filters**: Products organized by categories (e.g., "API", "Packages", "Themes") and filterable by price, rating, developer, and compatibility.
*   **Search Functionality**: Powerful search with keywords, tags, and advanced options.
*   **Product Pages**: Detailed pages for each product, including:
    *   Comprehensive descriptions, screenshots, and video demos.
    *   Pricing and package options.
    *   Customer reviews and ratings.
    *   Developer information and support channels.
    *   Technical specifications and compatibility.

## 4.2. The Purchase Process: Secure and simple checkout.

A smooth and secure checkout experience is crucial for customer satisfaction.

*   **Shopping Cart**: Customers can add multiple products/packages to their cart.
*   **Guest Checkout**: Option for guest checkout, with encouragement to register for future purchases and license management.
*   **User Registration/Login**: Seamless integration for existing users to log in or new users to register during checkout.
*   **Payment Gateways**: Integration with major payment providers like Stripe and PayPal for secure transactions.
*   **Order Confirmation**: Immediate confirmation of purchase, including order details and instructions for accessing products/licenses.
*   **Invoice Generation**: Automated generation and delivery of invoices.

## 4.3. The Customer Dashboard: Managing licenses, downloads, and subscriptions.

A dedicated customer dashboard will provide a centralized hub for managing all purchased products and services.

*   **My Purchases**:
    *   List of all purchased products.
    *   Access to download digital products (for one-time purchases).
    *   View order history and invoices.
*   **My Licenses**:
    *   Overview of all active, expired, and pending licenses.
    *   Display of license keys.
    *   Details of license terms (e.g., expiration date, usage limits).
    *   Option to renew expiring licenses.
    *   Ability to manage domain/IP restrictions for specific licenses.
*   **My Subscriptions**:
    *   Management of active subscriptions (SaaS products, recurring packages).
    *   Option to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel subscriptions.
    *   View next billing date and payment history.
*   **API Usage (for API Products)**:
    *   Real-time monitoring of API usage against defined limits.
    *   Graphs and reports on API call volume, bandwidth, and error rates.
    *   Notifications for approaching or exceeded usage limits.
*   **Support**:
    *   Direct access to developer support channels.
    *   Ability to open and track support tickets.
    *   [**Detailed Help and Support System & Chat Service**](./03-6-support-system.md)
*   **Account Settings**:
    *   Manage personal information, billing details, and payment methods.
    *   Change password and security settings.
    *   Manage notification preferences.
